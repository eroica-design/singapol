K STADIUM

Community-led,
DAO-based Company Builder

K STADIUM is a DAO Company Builder established in Singapore in 2022.
We invest in various crypto projects to create and expand K STADIUM’s unique ecosystem
through the blockchain mainnet and community-based Investment DAO.

K STADIUM APP

K STADIUM App is a platform that provides financial services, and it is a blockchain-based wallet for K STADIUM users. Governance participants get hances to vote for consensus and receive rewards for their investment participation.

K STADIUM BRIDGE

K STADIUM Bridge is a service that supports asset transfer between platforms. Assets issued on other platforms can be transferred to the K STADIUM mainnet using this bridge.

K STADIUM EXPLORER

K STADIUM Explorer records transactions in the blockchain transparently. It allows users to view and search transactions more clearly.

K STADIUM FORUM

K STADIUM Forum is a community that supports free communication among members. It serves as a forum for posting notices, sharing information, and discussing governance issues.

COMPLIANCE

K STADIUM Compliance Committee

The K STADIUM Compliance Committee supports the establishment of an organizational culture
in which executives and employees voluntarily observe and practice compliance management
In addition, for the continuous and healthy development of the company, we are expanding the base of
our activities in various ways, such as establishing a compliance management system, laying the foundation
for responsible management, and preventing risks in advance by responding to global regulations.

Compliance Structure

The K STADIUM Compliance Committee presents standards for compliance management
and conducts compliance audits so that the company’s goals can be carried out fairly and legally.
Through these efforts, K STADIUM strives to strengthen its compliance management
to the level of global standards.

Compliance Role

The K STADIUM Compliance Committee provides guidelines for internal rules
and processes for compliance management to ensure
that the company’s goals can be fairly and legally achieved.
In addition, for corporate risk management, we continuously monitor relevant laws
and regulations to identify and manage potential risks.
